Learn. Collaborate. Create.

Tech Retreat brings together high school students from all fields of study for a two-day event held in the heart of Canada's technology hub.

Ultimately, our goal is to encourage students, not only in the tech field, but all talented individuals to learn and collaborate in building real world applications. Participants will also hear from seasoned speakers and students in the industry.

Questions? Sponsorship or media enquiries? Get in touch!

The Event

Hackathon

Create something cool in a team of up to four.

This is where participants can build any software and hardware project of their choice. At the end, each team will be given the chance to showcase their project to other particpants and judges for a chance to win one or more of the top prizes. There will be mentors and volunteers to assist you at every step of the way.

Learnathon

Join us and learn about technology.

Participate in workshops with like-minded peers to learn about software and hardware programming in a fun casual atmosphere. Get the opportunity to talk to leaders from the industry, and upper-year university students currently completing math and engineering degrees.

And don't worry about food and drinks—it's on us.

FAQs

Who can attend?

Any high school student may apply, but only accepted students may attend.

When/where is Tech Retreat happening?

Tech Retreat will run on the weekend of July 9-10 at St. Paul’s College, which is located at Canada’s most innovative school—The University of Waterloo.

How much does this cost?

Nothing! It's absolutely free. Food will also be provided.

What food is provided?

Saturday's lunch and dinner along with Sunday's breakfast and lunch will be provided by us. Also, we will have snacks available throughout the weekend.

What should I bring?

A laptop and charger, reusable water bottle, government photo ID (if you're signing out hardware), and your brilliant mind. Feel free to bring a sleeping bag, toothbrush, and other basic amenities for this overnight event.

When do I apply?

Applications will open in April. Leave your email with us above and we'll notify you when applications are open. For now, keep up to date by heading over to our Facebook and Twitter.
