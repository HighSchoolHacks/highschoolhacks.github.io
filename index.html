Hackathon

Join us on November 14-15 to create something cool with a team up to 4 people (with at least 1 engineering student). Anyone can apply but only accepted students may attend.

A hackathon is an event where participants can work on any software and hardware project of their choosing without distraction. At the end of the competition you will be given the chance to present your project to the audience for a chance at winning one of the various prizes. It's an event where you can focus on building something cool - and we're all smart, creative people that know how to build things.

And don't worry about food and drinks - It's on us.

FAQs

Can I come?

Any student may apply (provided each team has at least one engineering student), but only accepted students may attend.

How much will this cost?

It's going to be $5 a person - we'll provide food and drinks.

What food are you providing?

We'll be providing food, snacks, drinks, and coffee throughout the event!

What should I bring to the event?

A laptop and charger, reusable water bottle, and your brilliant mind. Of course, feel free to bring things that can help you - earbuds, a whiteboard, and extra snacks (we'll supply some as well)!

What do I need to know about teams?

Each team has to have at least one engineering student. Teams can be formed informally - we'll have more info about team registration closer to the event.

When can I apply?

We'll be releasing more information and an application form in the coming weeks.
