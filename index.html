Waterloo Tech Retreat

August 15, 2015 | Waterloo, Ontario

The Waterloo Tech Retreat is a one-day event held in the heart of Canada's technology hub for high-school students.

Learn and collaborate with like-minded peers to create something amazing.

No experience? No problem. We've got mentors here to help you out, every step of the way!

Questions? Email us, or shoot us a tweet!

FAQs

Q: Who can come?

A: High school students, or if you just recently graduated from high school and would like to attend before university!

Q: As a high school student, why do I want to come to this retreat?

A: You’ll also be spending time with awesome people who you’ll get to meet and work with. It's also a great chance to learn a bit about university life. That's all we can say for now though, so leave your email with us and stay posted!

Q: How much is this going to cost?

A: It's going to be absolutely free! Food will be provided.

Q: I’m not sure what I like about technology, or if I like it at all - should I come to this event?

A: This event is a great way to discover more about technology. It's only one day, so it's a good chance to discover something new and different. We made this event so any high school student can come - regardless of experience. There will be mentors to help you out along the way as well.
