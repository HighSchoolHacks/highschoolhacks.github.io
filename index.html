Learn. Collaborate. Create.

The Waterloo Tech Retreat brings together passionate high school students for a one-day event held in the heart of Canada's technology hub. Hear from seasoned speakers and students in the industry. Learn and collaborate with like-minded peers to create something amazing.

Venue

Engineering 5, University of Waterloo

Questions? Get in touch!

Hackathon

Join us on August 15th from 8:30 AM to 8:00 PM to create something cool with a team up to 4 people. Anyone can apply but only accepted students may attend.

A hackathon is an event where participants can work on any software and hardware project of their choosing without distraction. At the end of the competition you will be given the chance to present your project to the audience for a chance at winning one of the various prizes. There will be mentors and volunteers to assist you at every step of the way.

Learnathon

Join us on August 15th from 8:30 AM to 8:00 PM to learn about technology! Anyone can apply but only accepted students may attend.

Participate in workshops with like-minded peers to learn about software and hardware programming in a fun casual atmosphere. Get the opportunity to talk to leaders from the industry, and upper-year university students completing math and engineering degrees!

And don't worry about food and drinks - It's on us.

FAQs

Can I come?

Any high school student may apply, but only accepted students may attend.

How much is this going to cost?

It's going to be absolutely free! Food will also be provided.

What food are you providing?

We'll be providing lunch, dinner, and snacks - so make sure you grab a bite of breakfast before. There will also be drinks!

What should I bring to the event?

A laptop and charger, reusable water bottle, government photo ID (if you're signing out hardware), and your brilliant mind. Of course, feel free to bring things that can help you - earbuds, a whiteboard, and extra snacks (we'll supply some as well)!

What about teams?

We'll send out team sign up forms to our accepted applicants.

Can I still apply?

Unfortunately, applications have closed. We'll be announcing more opportunities on Facebook and Twitter!

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes, check it out!
