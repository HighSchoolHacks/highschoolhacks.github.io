The Waterloo Tech Retreat brings together passionate high school students at a one-day event held in the heart of Canada's technology hub. Hear from seasoned speakers and students in the industry. Learn and collaborate with like-minded peers to create something amazing.

Venue

FAQs

Q: Who can come?

A: High school students, or if you just recently graduated from high school and would like to attend before university!

Q: I have no/little experience with technology - can I still come?

A: Tech Retreat focuses on having fun, learning, and collaborating. We're actually designing it so that students with no experience will have an awesome time! If you get stuck on something, don't worry about it - there will be mentors that can help.

Q: As a high school student, why do I want to come to this retreat?

A: You’ll also be spending time with awesome people who you’ll get to meet and work with. It's also a great chance to learn a bit about university life. That's all we can say for now though, so leave your email with us and stay posted!

Q: How much is this going to cost?

A: It's going to be absolutely free! Food will be provided.
