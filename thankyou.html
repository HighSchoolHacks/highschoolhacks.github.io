Thank you from Tech Retreat!

Hey hackers and learners,


Hope you all had lots of fun at the first ever Tech Retreat! We had a great time hosting the event and absolutely loved watching all of you build your pianos, tanks and hacks. Many sleepless nights were had, but we’re glad many of you enjoyed the experience we worked so hard to make.

The feedback we've heard from hackers and learners alike have been overwhelmingly positive. We can't wait to see what Tech Retreat will be moving forward.

If you had a good chat with a sponsor or won one of the sponsor prizes, please reach out to them and thank them (you can tweet at them too!) They were incredibly supportive and truly helped make the event possible.

Also, our awesome photographers, Martin and Karel, have uploaded their photos for you to see! For more updates, please follow us on Facebook and Twitter.

Oh, and if you missed out on hacks that were made in the hackathon, you can find them here. We're also keeping TechTanks online indefinitely, and will be developing it to be even better!
