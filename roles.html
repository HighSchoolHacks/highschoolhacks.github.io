Tech Retreat Organizer Roles

Thanks for expressing interest in becoming an organizer for Tech Retreat 2016! Below, we've provided some details on each of the teams for you to choose from (or suggest). If you have any other questions, feel free to connect with us on Facebook, Twitter, or via email.

Please note that Tech Retreat will take place in July 2016. You'll need to commit a few hours (more as the event approaches) weekly.

Logistics

Ordering and managing supplies, food, and swag; preparing the schedule and organization of the opening and closing ceremonies.

Learnathon

Creating the content for the learnathon component of Tech Retreat. Last year, we ran a software and hardware stream. This year, we're looking into expanding the number of streams and the content we cover. The team will also be defining the acceptance criteria for the learnathon.

Hackathon

Any hackathon related content. Organizers on this team will be responsible for defining acceptance criteria for the hackathon and finding judges.

Events

The events team organizes challenges and mini-events for all participants. They are also responsible for the speakers who will talk at Tech Retreat.

Sponsorship

The sponsorship team is responsible for raising funds from companies and organizations to fund Tech Retreat.

Public Relations

Talking to media and raising awareness of Tech Retreat.

Design

Designers will be creating assets for Tech Retreat and will work on building great user experiences.

Volunteer Coordinators

Volunteer Coordinators will be focusing on recruiting and organizing volunteers.

Community

Community team members will reach out to high school students, teachers, and administrators to advertise Tech Retreat. They will also manage our social media and email.

Other

Thought of something we missed? No problem, just let us know here - with some detail, please!
